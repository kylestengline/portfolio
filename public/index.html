My Skills

In my pursuit to change the world, I've gained experience in a variety of programming languages. The first of which was through working at IDT Corporation on the QA Team. I worked on proprietary projects, implementing new feature specs to test in-house software on both mobile and web applications (Ruby on Rails, Cucumber, Capybara, Calabash, Bash Scripting, Agile Methodologies, Jenkins, Jira).

I also worked within a team at a startup, PeduL, to implement new features on their subscription page (HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery). I also helped build their MVP which allows prospective college students to create campaigns where users can then donate to students to help alleviate college debt (React, Firebase).

The pursuit of happiness then brought me to an insurance company, Berkshire Hathaway Guard Insurance. Here, I updated crystal reports to a more modern programming language (C# .NET) and wrote/updated long SQL queries and functions to retrieve insurance records (T-SQL).

I then moved to Irvine, CA, where I worked for a company called Swrve. At Swrve, I configured API's, tested in-app-messaging, push and email notifications and ticketed bugs on the various Swrve products before release (RSpec, Capybara, SitePrism, Selenium, Agile Methodologies, Jira).

I now live in San Diego, CA working as a ServiceNow Developer at Bridgepoint Education. At Bridgepoint, I'm the sole developer supporting 3,500 users and five instances. I automate processes to improve user experience, design custom applications and workflows for multiple departments. My favorite part is writing scripts and leading project initiatives from development to production (ServiceNow, JavaScript, Scripting).
